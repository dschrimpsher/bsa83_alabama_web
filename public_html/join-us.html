Why Scouting

Some boys avoid obstacles. Boy Scouts overcome them. They live to display their abilities while learning new skills.

Boy Scouting is a year-round program for boys in fifth grade through high school that provides fun, adventure, learning, challenge, and responsibility to help them become the best person they can be.

In Boy Scouting, young men go places, test themselves, and have one-of-a-kind adventures that can’t be found anywhere else.

Boy Scouting is designed to be experienced outdoors. Hiking, camping, mountain biking, skateboarding, BMX, mountain climbing, kayaking, whitewater rafting—all of these and more are among those experiences. There is something for everyone in Scouting!
